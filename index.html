📏 Hook Scoring Rubric

🔴 Must have (5pt each)

  • Readable in 3 seconds who the target is
  • Has the right trigger word for the segment
  • No context needed to understand
  • Immediately sparks a question in the reader's head

🟡 Should have (3pt each)

  • Has a concrete number (%, minutes, people)
  • Has a personal angle (I / you / team)
  • Has contrast (before/after)
  • Platform-native format

🟢 Bonus B2B (2pt each)

  • Has ROI / a concrete dollar figure
  • Targets a decision-maker role
  • Controversial / polarizing
  • Specific Vietnam context

⚫ Auto-reject if

  • Generic ("AI is the future")
  • Unclear whether target is B2B or B2C
  • Too long (>15 words for FB)
  • Unnecessary jargon
✅ Threshold: A hook needs ≥ 25/35 points before testing with $15. Below threshold → rewrite first. B2B hooks need extra ROI clarity to pass LinkedIn ad review.

💰 Ads Brief — Kill / Scale Decision Rules

Check 48h after posting · Log to A/B Decision Log · Decide immediately — don't let it sit past 72h

ER Threshold Rules — Facebook/IG
TriggerAction
ER > 5% after 48h✅ BOOST — extend $50 × 7 days. Use the winning hook for a lookalike ad set. Flag for W3 scaling.
ER 3–5% after 48h⏸️ HOLD — don't extend, don't kill. Watch another 24h. No improvement → stop entirely.
ER < 3% after 48h❌ KILL — stop immediately. Rewrite the hook from the Hook Library. Hook score < 25/35 = rewrite before retesting. Don't throw more budget at it.
Reach < 300 after 24h⚠️ REVIEW — audience too narrow or ad got rejected. Check Ads Manager. Widen targeting or edit the creative.

TriggerAction
CTR > 1.5% LI✅ EXTEND — boost $20 × 5 days. Note DMs within 48h — follow up right away with the Corporate pitch.

CTR < 0.8% LI❌ STOP BOOST — leave it organic. Don't throw more LI budget at this post.
Scale Budget Rules — Winners
